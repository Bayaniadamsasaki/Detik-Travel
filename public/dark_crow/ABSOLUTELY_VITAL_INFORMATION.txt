Dark Crow is a true brush script font, hand-painted in 2020. 

Its top quality design with multiple ligatures combined make the lettering truly realistic. Use it for a poster, a product headline or in any context where you need an original, dynamic and eye-catching phrase.

Provided as upright and italic, and as a bonus a swash font. 

It has an extensive lingual support, covering all European Latin scripts. 
The font contains all characters you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers.
